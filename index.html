GREEN HELL VR

Green Hell VR is an Open World Survival Experience set in the Amazon rainforest, based on a successful PC game Green Hell.​ You are left alone in the jungle with no food or equipment. To survive, you must learn specific survival techniques like building shelters and crafting tools such as weapons. To hunt and defend yourself can be a matter of life and death.

The game faithfully recreates the dangerous conditions of the Amazon Jungle. You will have to face your weaknesses, as well as hunger, thirst, fatigue, wild animals, tropical diseases and deteriorating mental health.

Story

You, the anthropologist, set a camp amid the Amazon rainforest. You are looking for a special herb which, according to some legends, can be found only on the land of the Waraha tribe. The herb could be a cure to almost any disease and, in particular, help your wife fight off the cancer that is killing her.

But something went wrong and you find yourself stranded in the dangerous jungle, surrounded by predators with only your trusty smartwatch, backpack and the survival guide grabbed just before leaving home.

Crafting

You are stranded in the Amazon with almost nothing, so crafting is your best friend. The VR experience makes crafting even more realistic as you need to find the right elements and put them together in a specific way to make tools or shelter.

Healing

Experiment with different potions to survive in the wild. You will discover herbs and their medical potential by mixing them, and making a brew over the fire. Be careful though, some herbs don’t go together, and some… well, you’ll see.

FOOD SOURCES

You don’t have to kill animals to survive. There are tasty and nutritious fruits like coconuts or bananas, a lot of mushrooms and plenty of herbs, bulbs and nuts. These are sources of healthy proteins, carbohydrates and fats.
